What Spontano is

Spontano lets people post and join real-life hangouts. We provide the platform; the meetups themselves are organized by users, not by us.

Your responsibilities

  • You must be at least 18 years old to use Spontano.
  • Post honestly — real plans, real times, real places.
  • Be respectful in chats and at meetups. No harassment, hate, spam, or illegal activity.
  • Don't use Spontano for commercial promotion, dating-for-pay, or recruiting into anything.

Meetups are at your own risk

We don't vet users or verify identities. You decide who to meet and where. Please read our safety guidelines. Spontano is not liable for what happens at or around meetups organized through the platform.

You own what you post. We may remove content or suspend accounts that break these terms, without notice.
