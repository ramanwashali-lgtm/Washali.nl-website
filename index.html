Good questions

Everything people usually want to know before their first hangout.

Is Spontano free?

Yes — posting and joining hangouts is completely free.

How does matching work?

You pick interests in your profile. Every hangout is scored against them, so the plans most like you rise to the top. "Surprise me" adds a bit of chance.

Is it safe to meet strangers?

Always meet in public places, tell a friend where you're going, and trust your gut. Read our safety guidelines before your first meetup.

Which cities does it work in?

Anywhere — every hangout is tagged with a city, and you can filter the feed to yours. The more people post in your city, the livelier it gets.

What if nobody joins my hangout?

It happens — post it anyway. Plans with a clear time, place and vibe get the most joins. You can edit or cancel any time.

Can I bring a friend?

Sure — open the hangout and tap "Share" to send them the link so they can grab a spot themselves.
